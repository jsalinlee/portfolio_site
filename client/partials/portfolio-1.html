Let's Shoot Rocks in Space

<
>

I worked with two of my bootcamp classmates to recreate classic Asteroids on iOS with a twist. Your ship's movement is controlled with motion controls, giving the game an addictive learning curve. We developed the game in 3 days for a SpriteKit hackathon and received 1st place. Before the hackathon, we had zero knowledge of SpriteKit and Core Motion. Our hackathon build featured a boss and an endless world, but were later scrapped for future plans. Currently, LSRIS is transitioning to the Unity game engine with plans of becoming a cooperative multiplayer dogfighting game.

    Technologies Used

  • Swift
  • SpriteKit
  • Core Motion
  • Xcode
