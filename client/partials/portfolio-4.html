Scavengers

<
>

A fun side project developed with two of my gaming friends. We played numerous survival games and decided to develop one with the elements we wanted and without the elements we disliked. Other than a few placeholder sprites, all of the content in Scavengers will be originally handcrafted. In Scavengers, you live in a desolate wasteland surrounded by abandoned spaceships, and hostile inhabitants. As you pick your way through scraps of worn technology, build your way up from rusty drones and sheet metal huts to fully-customizable walker mechs and a respectable home base.
