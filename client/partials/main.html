About Me

Hey there! A little background on how I came to be a programmer: A couple of years ago, I was diagnosed with clinical depression and it held me back for quite a while. I spent several years spinning my wheels in school, trying to pursue a Computer Science degree. I was amazed by what could be built with programming, but had little love for school and theory. I just wanted to build. After a long year of therapy, medication, and self-reconstruction, I decided to drop out of school and apply to a coding bootcamp. After 3 1/2 hard months of coding, I am even more amazed by what programmers have done, and excited to finally have the tools to join in on the fun.

I am highly adaptable to new technologies, calm and collected under stress, and ready for a good puzzle.

My name is Jonathan Salin Lee and I am an up-and-coming developer, ready to make a mark in the programming industry. Whether that mark is big or small, my dream is to bring a little joy to the world.
