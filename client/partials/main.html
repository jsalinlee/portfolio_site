About Me

Hey there! A brief background on how I became a programmer: For most of my life, I struggled in academia. I had a good head on my shoulders, but something about mundane classrooms and repetitive busy work couldn't inspire me. As a gamer, I was amazed by the worlds and experiences that could be built with programming, but had little love for school and theory. By the time college came around, I realized that school was just stalling me from pursuing what I really wanted. I wanted to build and I wanted to build now. After years of wasted time and money, I decided to drop out of school and attend a coding bootcamp. That's my class you see up there! After 3 1/2 hard months of coding, I am even more amazed by what programmers have done and excited to finally join in on the fun.

I am highly adaptable to new technologies, calm and collected under stress, and ready for a good puzzle.

My name is Jonathan Salin Lee and I am an up-and-coming developer, ready to make a mark in the programming industry. Whether that mark is big or small, my dream is to bring a little joy to the world.
