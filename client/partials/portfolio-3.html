GainBoy is a health and fitness app that uses gaming philosophies to add an extra element of accountability and motivation to working out. GainBoy is heavily inspired by the life-management website, Habitica. I originally developed GainBoy for personal use, but I will be polishing it up to be deployed on the App Store. In GainBoy, you are transformed into an RPG character with all the bells and whistles including health, experience, and gold. You can log workouts into your Adventure Log and track your weight, personal records, and more on your Character Sheet. Some tentative features of GainBoy include a section that pulls up an exercise API for easy exercise selection and a Shop section for users to create their own rewards to work towards.
