Tamagotchi

<
>

Another hackathon winner with the same team. This time, we had 24 hours and were required to include MapKit. Tamagotchi is inspired by the virtual pet device of the same name and some mechanics of Pokemon Go. In our version of Tamagotchi, you have a little critter that lives and thrives under your care. Over time, it will get hungry and bored and will need berries and some playtime to keep its spirits up. The game populates the world with berries for you to explore and collect and maybe even a rare toy!

    Technologies Used

  • Swift
  • SpriteKit
  • MapKit
  • Core Data
  • Xcode
